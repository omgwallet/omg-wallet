Buy Rules

📌 How to Buy OMG Coins

  • ✅ Select any buy offer from the Buy section
  • ✅ Pay the exact amount to the displayed UPI ID (9‑minute timer starts)
  • ✅ After successful payment, copy the UTR / Transaction ID
  • ✅ Paste the UTR in the payment confirmation screen
  • ✅ Your order will change to "Awaiting Verification"
  • ✅ Admin verifies the payment within 2‑3 hours
  • ✅ Once verified, bonus + OMG coins are credited instantly
  • ✅ If UTR is not submitted within 9 minutes, the order auto‑cancels
  • ⚠️ Double-check UTR before submitting. Incorrect UTR cannot be corrected.

Sell Rules

📌 How to Sell OMG Coins

  • ✅ Go to Sell / Withdraw section
  • ✅ Add your withdrawal account (PhonePe / Paytm / Bank)
  • ✅ Enter the amount you wish to sell (minimum ₹150)
  • ✅ Select your saved account
  • ✅ Confirm the sell with your 4‑digit PIN
  • ✅ Order status becomes "Processing"
  • ✅ Money is usually transferred within 30 minutes to 4 hours
  • ✅ Do not sell more than your available balance
  • ⚠️ Double-check account details. Incorrect details cannot be reversed.

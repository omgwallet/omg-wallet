📥 Buy Rules

How to Buy OMG Coins

  • Select any buy offer from the Buy section
  • Pay the exact amount to the displayed UPI ID
  • After successful payment, copy the UTR / Transaction ID
  • Paste the UTR in the payment confirmation screen
  • Order will expire in 9 minutes if UTR not submitted
  • Bonus coins (4% or 10%) will be added automatically after confirmation
  • Usually confirmed within 1-24 hours

📤 Sell Rules

How to Sell OMG Coins

  • Enter the amount you want to sell
  • Choose your withdrawal method (PhonePe, Paytm or Bank)
  • Fill correct details (Phone Number or Bank Account + IFSC)
  • Confirm the sell with your 4-digit PIN
  • Sell order status will show as "Processing"
  • Money is usually transferred within 30 minutes to 4 hours
  • Do not sell more than your available balance
